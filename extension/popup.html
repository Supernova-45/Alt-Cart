alt+cart Help

What alt+cart does

alt+cart opens product pages in an accessible format with fit analysis, return risk, image descriptions, and text-to-speech support.

Extension features

  • Open in alt+cart — Send the current page to alt+cart (button above or keyboard shortcut)
  • Hover to speak — Hold Alt and hover over images on any page to hear their descriptions read aloud

Keyboard shortcuts

Alt+Shift+P
Open current tab in alt+cart
